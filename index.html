Cheers to giving back with an Alfie's List Drink Pass

Are you ready to spend the summer at your favorite brewery with your best furry friend? With an Alfie's List Drink Pass, you get BOGO drinks at 23 dog-friendly breweries and distilleries around the Twin Cities.

Now through May 31, get $5 off all Alfie's List Drink Passes -- but that's not the best part! $5 from every pass purchased also goes directly to Animal Humane Society to help give more animals the second chances they deserve.

Donate pet supplies to help pet parents in your community

From pet food to cat litter to flea/tick preventatives, the Community Outreach program at AHS has a consistent need for pet supplies – and that need is even larger in the summer.

Now through June 9, AHS is hosting a Community Outreach Supply Drive to gather necessary resources to support more pets and their families in our community. Drop off new or gently used pet supplies at any of our three shelter locations during business hours.
